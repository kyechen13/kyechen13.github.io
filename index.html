Graphene Heated Eye Mask

Graphene + Heating, Instant Eyes Relief

OUR STORY

Why

We Design

The Creation of

iQmax® Graphene Heated Eye Mask

-

The advancement of technology has created a habit of staring at a screen, with an average of 4 hours screen time per day. This induces more stress on human’s eye, causing fatigue and dryness, and has slowly become a common disease in modern era.

Expert suggested that the proper eye compress can soothe eyes and provide an instant relief. iQmax® Graphene Heated Eye Mask is created as a mean for you to chill your eyes effectively, by utilizing functional textiles with enhanced heating performance. Relax anywhere and anytime, spend 10 minutes and brighten your day with this!


More than

Heating

Graphene is a remarkable material, it has multitude of astonishing properties, earning the title “Wonder Material”, it is two hundred times stronger than steel and has remarkable heat transmission, better than any material in the world!


iQmax® Graphene Heated Eye Mask utilizes graphene’s heat transmission and far infrared heating, combined with our advanced heating module, creating a stronger heat penetration and temperature rise that can improve blood circulation around eyes, subsequently creating an effective solution to relieve eye strain, reduce dark circles and red eyes.

Easy to

Maintain

Apart from functional aspect, we pay great attention for ease of maintenance

-

Because human’s eye are prone to release fatty substance (sebum), that leads to bacterial proliferation when you use any heated eye mask. To counter this, the inner layer of our eye mask uses antibacterial fabrics that are also breathable and skin-friendly, also the whole mask can be washed either by hand or machine wash, designed for you to maintain it without hassle!

WARMING YOURS EYES

Are you sure you know how to warm compress your eyes?


The ideal product to warm compress your eyes should be dry, constant-temperature settings and has timer setting.


Avoid heating your eyeballs directly to reduce damage caused by dry eyes.


Set the temperature at around 40-50°C, 10 minutes per day and avoid prolonged use or excessively high temperature use, to prevent eye’s dehydration.
