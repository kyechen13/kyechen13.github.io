With more than 40 years of experience, Haur Shiang specializes in the sales and development of various textile machine components suitable for staple fiber, filament, and non-woven fabrics. 豪祥實業股份有限公司成立於 1974 年，在業界靠著專業誠信的製造與銷售經驗而屹立不搖，堅持提供客戶優良服務，建立長久信譽。我們主要業務為進出口零組件銷售以及製造高品質機械設備配件，更為歐洲重要紡織機品牌及零件的獨家代理。

Haur Shiang Ind., Co., Ltd. was established in 1974. It stands steady in the industry by relying on professional and honest manufacturing and sales experience, insisting on providing customers with excellent service and establishing long-term reputation. Our main business is the sales of import, export, and manufacture of high-quality textile component. We are also the exclusive European brand agency for textile machines and spare parts.
